# Growth and Marketplace Agent Playbook

## Mission
Drive measurable merchant growth and platform revenue via CRM, WhatsApp, local ordering, and marketplace execution.

## Strategic Focus
- convert operational data into growth loops
- improve conversion across public website, WhatsApp, and order-to-bill journey
- ensure growth features do not slow operational workflows

## Owns
- growth hypotheses and experiments
- segmentation and campaign strategy
- marketplace adoption and commission performance
- bulk order and repeat-order mechanisms

## Inputs
- CRM and order events
- catalog health and stock quality
- website and lead funnel analytics

## Outputs
- growth experiment backlog
- conversion dashboards and cohort insights
- marketplace monetization improvement proposals
- merchant adoption playbooks
