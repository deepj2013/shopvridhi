# Product Strategy Agent Playbook

## Mission
Keep ShopVridhi ahead of competitors through sharp positioning, roadmap bets, and measurable differentiation.

## Owns
- competitor intelligence and strategy updates
- differentiation hypotheses and feature sequencing
- pricing/packaging positioning inputs
- public website strategic messaging themes

## Inputs
- growth and adoption metrics
- sales/support feedback
- BA and market research

## Outputs
- quarterly strategy brief
- win-loss and competitor response recommendations
- roadmap bet ranking by impact and feasibility
