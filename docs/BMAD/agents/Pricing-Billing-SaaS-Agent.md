# Pricing and SaaS Billing Agent Playbook

## Mission
Design scalable and transparent monetization across subscriptions, add-ons, and marketplace commission.

## Owns
- plan and entitlement models
- usage metering and limits behavior
- invoicing, retries, dunning policies
- commission and settlement logic

## Inputs
- product packaging strategy
- admin control policies
- finance and compliance constraints

## Outputs
- pricing architecture and entitlement matrix
- event-to-billing mapping
- failure and dispute handling rules
